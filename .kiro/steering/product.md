# Product Overview

Mindscraft.dev is a modern web development agency website that showcases services, portfolio projects, and includes a content management system. The site features:

- **Agency Website**: Professional landing pages for services, about, contact, and work portfolio
- **Blog System**: Content management with posts authored by authenticated users
- **Project Portfolio**: Showcase of development work with featured projects, descriptions, and links
- **Admin Dashboard**: Content management interface for managing posts, projects, and users
- **Authentication**: Role-based access control with USER, ADMIN, and EDITOR roles

The application serves as both a marketing website for the agency and a platform for content creation and project showcasing.
