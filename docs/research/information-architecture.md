```

├── Services - Service overview
```

### Current Content Gaps Identified

#### 1. **AI Specialization Not Prominent**
- Current hero focuses on generic "Digital Products"
- No clear positioning as AI-first development partner
- Missing AI-specific case studies and capabilities

#### 2. **Startup Focus Unclear**
- Generic messaging for "startups and enterprises"
- No startup-specific service packages or pricing
- Missing startup success stories and testimonials

#### 3. **Trust Building Elements Scattered**
- Testimonials separated from relevant service context
- No clear credentialing or team expertise showcase
- Missing client results and business impact metrics

#### 4. **Conversion Path Optimization Needed**
- Multiple CTAs without clear hierarchy
- No lead magnets or value-driven offers
- Missing consultation or discovery process explanation

## Competitive Analysis Insights

### Best Practices from Competitor Analysis

#### **Toptal** - Clear Value Hierarchy
- Immediate value proposition: "Top 3% of talent"
- Process explanation: How they vet and match talent
- Social proof integrated throughout user journey

#### **Clay** - Case Study-Driven Architecture
- Portfolio-first approach with detailed case studies
- Process transparency with methodology explanation
- Client results prominently featured

#### **Designlab** - Educational Content Strategy
- Learning-focused content architecture
- Clear progression paths for different user types
- Community and success story integration

### Key Architectural Patterns to Adopt

1. **Value Proposition Clarity**: Immediate understanding of unique positioning
2. **Process Transparency**: Clear explanation of how you work
3. **Results-Focused Content**: Business impact and metrics prominently featured
4. **Educational Approach**: Thought leadership and knowledge sharing
5. **Trust Building Integration**: Credentials and social proof throughout journey
