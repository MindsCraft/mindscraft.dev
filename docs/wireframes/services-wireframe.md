## Key Design Decisions

### Service Presentation
- **Clear Value Props**: Each service leads with business benefits
- **Specific Deliverables**: Detailed list of what's included
- **Transparent Pricing**: Upfront pricing ranges with timelines
- **Social Proof**: Links to relevant case studies for each service

### User Journey Optimization
- **Service Discovery**: Easy comparison between different offerings
- **Decision Support**: FAQ section addresses common concerns
- **Multiple CTAs**: Different engagement levels (learn more, get quote, contact)
- **Trust Building**: Technology stack and process transparency

### Content Strategy
- **Benefit-Focused**: Features explained in terms of business value
- **Startup-Specific**: Pricing and timelines appropriate for startup budgets
- **Technical Credibility**: Technology stack shows expertise
- **Process Transparency**: Clear explanation of how we work

### Conversion Optimization
- **Multiple Price Points**: Options for different budget levels
- **Clear Next Steps**: Obvious path from interest to engagement
- **Risk Reduction**: FAQ addresses common objections
- **Contact Options**: Multiple ways to get in touch
