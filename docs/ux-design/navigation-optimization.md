### A/B Testing Opportunities

#### Navigation Tests
- **Services Dropdown**: Problem-based vs. Technology-based categories
- **CTA Buttons**: "Start Project" vs. "Get Quote" vs. "Schedule Call"
- **Mobile Menu**: Slide-out vs. Full-screen overlay

#### Content Tests
- **Headlines**: Benefit-focused vs. Feature-focused
- **Case Study Presentation**: Metrics-first vs. Story-first
- **Pricing Display**: Ranges vs. "Starting at" vs. "Contact for pricing"

### Accessibility Improvements

#### Navigation Accessibility
- **Keyboard Navigation**: Tab order follows logical flow
- **Screen Reader Support**: Proper ARIA labels and landmarks
- **Focus Indicators**: Clear visual focus states
- **Skip Links**: "Skip to main content" option

#### Content Accessibility
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive alt text for all images
- **Color Contrast**: WCAG AA compliance (4.5:1 ratio)
- **Font Sizes**: Minimum 16px for body text
- **Link Context**: Clear link purposes without surrounding text
